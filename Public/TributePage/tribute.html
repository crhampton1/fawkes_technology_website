Steve Jobs

The man who changed the world.


Jobs unveiling the iPhone 4 at the 2010 Worldwide Developers Conference

About:

  • 1955: Steve Jobs born.
  • 1961: Jobs family moves to Mountian View, California.
  • 1968: Jobs calls Bill Hewlet for parts to a frequency counter. He recieves the parts and an internship.
  • 1970:  Steve Jobs meets Steve Wozniak. A friendship is formed.
  • 1972: Graduates from High School. Attends one year officially of college, but after dropping out continues to sit in on calligraphy classes. This helped form the beautiful typefaces of the computer.
  • 1976: Co-Founds Apple.
  • 1984: Steve debuts the Macintosh, the first commercially successful small computer with a GUI.
  • 1985: Jobs is outed from Apple. Forms NeXT Computer.
  • 1986: Buys the Graphics Groups division of Lucasfilm and forms Pixar Animation Studios.
  • 1997: After Apple buys NeXT for $429 million, Steve returns to Apple.
  • 1998: Introduces the iMac.
  • 2001: Introduces iTunes and iPod.
  • 2007: Introduces iPhone.
  • 2009: Takes six month leave of absence for medical reasons.
  • 2011: Resigns from CEO in August and dies in October.
"For the past 33 years, I have looked in the mirror every morning and asked myself: 'If today were the last day of my life, would I want to do what I am about to do today?' And whenever the answer has been 'No' for too many days in a row, I know I need to change something."
-Steve Jobs
